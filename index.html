Welcome to the Hololive Fan Wiki!![edit source]

Hololive Production (Japanese: ホロライブプロダクション Hororaibu Purodakushon) or simply known as Hololive (Japanese: ホロライブ Hororaibu), is an agency of Virtual YouTubers created by Cover Corp. As of December 2021 there are now 62 talents under Hololive and its branches (35 Hololive, 9 Holostars, 1 INNK Music, 6 Hololive ID, and 11 Hololive EN).
